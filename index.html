Projects and Other related activities

My first SaaS Product (that I couldn't sell)

The product was a video to report transcription tool that at first I needed for myself for my studies. Inputs are lecture recordings and outputs are word reports for each lecture that is consisted of transcription of the lecture and the slides that are shown during the lecture. Slides are detected by image recognition algorithms of OpenCV and transcription is done by "whisper" package from OpenAI. They are linked by timestamps and everything is combined into a one word document in the end that is generated by Python using the "python-docx" package. My hope was to sell it to students at KU Leuven but turns out, marketing and sales are quite difficult, even harder than building the product :)

Master Thesis - Scraping Immoweb

This work is part of my master thesis at KU Leuven. The objective was to scrape immoweb.be and create a dataset from the listings that will be used in EPC estimation and price prediction models. The scraper is written in Python and only by using requests package since data is retrieved by API calls. Each day around ~1500 listings are added to the SQLite Database and currently >120,000 unique listings are stored. Scraper is automated and runs daily on my local computer.

Predicting Michelin restaurants' cuisine using Deep Learning

The objective was to predict type of cuisine of a Michelin starred restaurant using pre labeled image data. It was a project of Big Data and Analytics course at KU Leuven. We've used Tensorflow and transfer learning from some pre-trained models(particularly Xception) on the image dataset. The course had 3 other projects similar to this that helped me acquire practical skills and modern tools in data science.

Scraping kotwijs to find accommodation in Leuven, 2022

Leuven is notorious for its housing market and I had difficulty in finding a house as well. Kotwijs is the official website of KU Leuven for student housing. I checked this website every day for 3 months. In the first month, it was manual and tedious. Then I decided automate it with a bot that checks for new listings and sends an email and push notification to me so I could be the first responder. I used Selenium because the website was dynamically loaded.

Beach Volley Field Reservation bot, 2022

Another real life problem that I could leverage technology to solve it. We were playing beach volley regularly back then but fields were always in high demand and got reserved in seconds, as soon as they were available. I know other people hated us but yes, robots are faster than humans. Great success!
